About Us

Who are We?

Padel Club is not Irland's largest padel hall, but we believe we can offer the country's most beautiful padel experience. If you are looking for a relaxed meeting place for all ages where everything is done with love, knowledge and maximum service, then you have found the right place!

What we are about

  • Fantastic atmosphere.
  • Making padel a sport for everyone.
  • Improving Body, Mind and Soul.
  • Our incredible members.
  • And last but not least, have FUN!
