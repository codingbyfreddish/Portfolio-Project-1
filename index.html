About Us

Who are We?

Padel Club is not Ireland's largest padel hall, but we believe we can offer the country's most enjoyable padel experience. If you are looking for a relaxed meeting place for all ages where everything is done with love, knowledge and maximum service, then you have found the right place!

What we are about

  • Fantastic atmosphere.
  • Showing that padel is a sport for everyone.
  • Improving Body, Mind and Soul.
  • Our incredible members.
  • And last but not least, having FUN!
